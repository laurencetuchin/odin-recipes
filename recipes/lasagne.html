Lasagne

Description

Indulge in the rich and savory layers of our classic lasagna recipe that promises to be a showstopper at your dinner table. This timeless Italian favorite is a symphony of flavors, featuring perfectly seasoned ground beef, sweet Italian sausage, and a medley of aromatic herbs, all nestled between delicate sheets of pasta. Our luscious ricotta and mozzarella cheese blend adds a creamy texture that melts in your mouth with every bite. Complemented by a robust marinara sauce made from vine-ripened tomatoes, garlic, and onions, this lasagna is a culinary masterpiece that captures the essence of comfort food. Baked to perfection, the golden and bubbling cheese on top will have your guests eagerly reaching for seconds. Whether you're hosting a family gathering or craving a cozy night in, our lasagna recipe is a delicious invitation to savor the joy of homemade goodness.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles (cooked according to package instructions)
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound sweet Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper (or to taste)
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook 9 lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a large skillet, brown 1 pound of ground beef and 1/2 pound of sweet Italian sausage (casings removed) over medium heat.
  3. Add finely chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet; saut√© until onions are translucent.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 20 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with a beaten egg.
  6. Preheat the oven to the temperature specified on the lasagna noodle package.
  7. Assemble the lasagna in a baking dish in the following layers: a thin layer of sauce, lasagna noodles, half of the ricotta mixture, a third of the mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses, and repeat. Finish with a layer of sauce and the remaining cheeses on top.
  8. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes.
  9. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  10. Allow the lasagna to cool for a few minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired.
  11. Serve and enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
