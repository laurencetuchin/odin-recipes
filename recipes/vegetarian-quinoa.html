Vegetarian Quinoa

Description

Embark on a delightful journey of flavors with our Vegetarian Quinoa Salad, a vibrant medley that celebrates freshness and wholesome goodness. Begin by simmering quinoa to a fluffy perfection, creating the ideal canvas for a symphony of ingredients. Chickpeas, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, red bell pepper, and red onion join forces, creating a colorful and nutrient-packed ensemble. Crumbled feta cheese and Kalamata olives add a burst of savory richness, while a zesty dressing of extra-virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper ties everything together in a harmonious blend. This salad is not only a visual feast but a celebration of diverse textures and flavors. Serve chilled, allowing the melding of ingredients to create a refreshing and satisfying experience. Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 can (15 ounces) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, sliced
  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, combine 1 cup of rinsed quinoa with 2 cups of water or vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover, and simmer until quinoa is cooked and water is absorbed.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, drained and rinsed chickpeas, diced cucumber, halved cherry tomatoes, diced red bell pepper, and finely chopped red onion.
  3. Add crumbled feta cheese and sliced Kalamata olives to the bowl.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together 3 tablespoons of extra-virgin olive oil, 2 tablespoons of red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Pour the dressing over the quinoa mixture and toss until well combined.
  6. Garnish the salad with fresh parsley.
  7. Serve the Vegetarian Quinoa Salad chilled and enjoy the delightful flavors and textures!
